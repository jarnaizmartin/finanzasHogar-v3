Architecture

Why no cloud?

Because your financial data is the most sensitive that exists. Your wealth, your debts, your spending habits. You shouldn't have to trust that an external server won't see it, won't sell it, won't lose it in a security breach.

Local-first means the app runs entirely on your device. No account to create, no server to connect to. Your data lives on your machine, encrypted with AES-GCM 256 + PBKDF2-SHA-256 with 200,000 iterations. The same standard as professional password managers.

The encryption code is public and auditable — it's one of our marketing arguments, not a vague promise.
